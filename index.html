The Original Beef of Chicagoland

Not quite a diner, not quite a deli, not quite a fast-food joint, it is a storefront establishment with big plate-glass windows, grubby in a reassuring way, with illuminated signs that advertise Italian beef or gyros.

A Quick View of Our Place

Located in the heart of Chicago, The Bear is a must-visit spot for any sandwich lover. The restaurant boasts a cozy, rustic atmosphere with an attractive view. The menu features a wide variety of sandwiches, each made with the freshest ingredients and creative flavor combinations. Standout options include the slow-roasted pulled beef with tangy coleslaw and the grilled eggplant with roasted red pepper and mozzarella. The staff is friendly and attentive, and the prices are reasonable. Make sure to save room for a homemade dessert, such as the warm apple crisp. Whether you're a local or just passing through, The Bear is sure to satisfy your sandwich cravings.

A Glimpse of the Menu

Italian Beef Sandwich is a popular dish that originated in Chicago, Illinois. This sandwich is made by slow-roasting beef that is then thinly sliced and served on a long roll with a variety of toppings, such as giardiniera, a spicy pickled vegetable mix, and mozzarella cheese. The sandwich is then typically dipped in the beef's own juices before being served.

Spaghetti is another classic Italian dish that is loved by many. It is made by cooking long, thin noodles in a flavorful tomato sauce and is often paired with meatballs or sausage. Risotto, on the other hand, is a type of rice dish that is cooked slowly in a broth or wine, making it creamy and flavorful. A Short-Ribbed Risotto can be made with a variety of ingredients, such as mushrooms, seafood, or vegetables.

When it comes to Italian cuisine, there is something for everyone. Each of those dishes has its own special flavor and texture that is sure to satisfy your taste buds.
